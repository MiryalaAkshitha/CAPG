Faq

Frequently Asked & Question

What is the difference between Tax Planning and Tax Avoidance?

Tax planning involves legal strategies to minimize tax liability, while tax avoidance is illegal and involves schemes to evade taxes.

How often should I get my books audited?

The frequency of audits depends on factors like company size, industry, and regulatory requirements. However, annual audits are common for most businesses.

What are the key benefits of hiring a Chartered Accountant?

Chartered Accountants provide expert advice on taxation, financial planning, business setup, compliance, and more. They help businesses save money, avoid penalties, and make informed decisions.
